Your server is ready. Here is a quick tutorial on how to make it public using Playit.gg.

Step 1: Go to the Dashboard and click ▶ Start to run your server for the first time.

Step 2: Look at the Server Plan panel on the Dashboard. Wait until you see a warning link that says Claim your tunnel IP →.

Step 3: Click that link. It will take you to the Playit.gg website.

Step 4: Log in or create a free Playit.gg account.

Step 5: Once you add the agent to your account, Playit.gg will give you a public IP address (e.g., auto-words.playit.gg) that you can share with your friends!

Tip: You can customize your subdomain and server region directly on the Playit.gg website dashboard!
