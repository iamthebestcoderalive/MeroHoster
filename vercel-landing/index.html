Direct Connections

Players connect directly to your PC using WebRTC. No intermediate proxy servers routing traffic across the globe, meaning significantly lower ping for your players.

Use Your Hardware

No RAM limits or subscription tiers. Use the hardware you already own to host your world exactly how you want.

Automatic Mod Sync

MeroClient automatically downloads and syncs your exact modpack to players on join. No more zipping folders.

Play With Friends

To achieve the lowest possible latency, MeroHoster establishes a true direct connection. This means your IP address is visible to connected players. It is designed for playing with trusted friends, not public communities.
